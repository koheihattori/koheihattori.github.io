Research

I am a astrophysicist working on stellar dynamics in the Milky Way.
I use the big data of the postion and velocity from Gaia (1.3 billion stasr) as well as the chemical information from many spectroscopi surveys.
In 2018, I discovered some hypervelocity star candidates from Gaia data, and I am developing a code to more efficiently find hypervelocity stars.
